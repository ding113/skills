> **Draft** — scope agreed, behavior not yet written. Do not rely on this skill yet.

The planned shape:

1. **Genre presets** — technical deep-dive, blog post, documentation, news report, newsletter — each with its own structure, register, and length guidance, for both Chinese and English.
2. **Drafting pass** — write for the chosen genre and language, sourcing concrete detail from the user's material instead of padding with generalities.
3. **Self-review system** — a checklist pass that hunts the tells of AI prose (formulaic openers and closers, hedging boilerplate, uniform sentence rhythm, listicle-itis, translationese in Chinese copy) and rewrites what it finds.
4. **Revision loop** — apply the review, re-check, stop when a pass finds nothing.
