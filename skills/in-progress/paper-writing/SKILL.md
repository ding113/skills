> **Draft** — scope agreed, behavior not yet written. Do not rely on this skill yet.

The planned shape:

1. **Skeleton first** — settle the claim, contributions, and section outline before any prose; every section knows what it must prove.
2. **Section drafting** — write in the venue's register (LaTeX or Markdown), grounding every statement in the user's results and cited sources — never inventing citations.
3. **Academic self-review** — a reviewer-style checklist pass: overclaiming, unsupported statements, undefined notation, missing related-work coverage.
4. **Revision loop** — apply review feedback (the user's or a real reviewer's) section by section, keeping the argument consistent end to end.
